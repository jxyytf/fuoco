OUR STORY

Founded in 2025, Fuoco Records began as a small collective of passionate music producers and artists. Today, we've grown into a full-service record label dedicated to discovering and nurturing exceptional talent across multiple genres.

The name "Fuoco" (Italian for "fire") represents our burning passion for music and our commitment to igniting the careers of talented artists. We provide the resources, guidance, and platform needed to turn raw talent into professional success.

OUR SERVICES

We provide comprehensive support to help artists at every stage of their career.

Record Production

From demo to master, our in-house producers and engineers will help you create professional-quality recordings.

Marketing & Promotion

Strategic campaigns to get your music heard by the right audiences across all platforms and media.

Artist Development

Personalized coaching and mentorship to help you grow as an artist and build a sustainable career.

Distribution

Worldwide digital distribution to all major platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, and more.

Booking & Touring

Access to our network of venues and promoters to help you perform live and connect with fans.

Royalty Management

Transparent accounting and collection of all royalties owed to you from streaming, syncs, and more.
